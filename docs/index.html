Features

Easy Access Call Reactions

Reaction buttons are now moved out of the popup menu and placed onto the call action bar to avoid accidentally raising your hand when you just want to react!


Notifications relocated

New message notifications no longer cover your message box. You can keep typing without waiting for the notification to disappear or needing to click on it.


Spotify player

Spotify links to songs, albums or artists can be played directly in the Teams window.


X/Twitter integration

Received a tweet? You can view it directly in your window without needing to click on it.


Youtube player

Somebody sent a link youtube video? You can play it right there without the need of leaving to Youtube anymore.


Instagram integration

You can view Instagram posts directly in your chat without needing to leave the window.

Privacy Policy
